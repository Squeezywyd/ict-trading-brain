# Market Structure - MOC

> **Purpose:** How to read the skeleton of price. Structure is the first filter — before any entry model, before any timing, you must know whether the market is going up, down, or reversing. Everything else is noise without this.

---

## The Building Blocks

The raw components that structure is constructed from.

- [[Swing High]] — price peaks; BSL pools sit above them; define bullish HH or bearish LH
- [[Swing Low]] — price troughs; SSL pools sit below them; define bullish HL or bearish LL
- [[Equal Highs]] — two or more swing highs at the same level; most visible BSL pool
- [[Equal Lows]] — two or more swing lows at the same level; most visible SSL pool
- [[Market Structure]] — how to combine swing points into a trend read (HH/HL or LH/LL)

---

## Structural Events

The candle-level events that change or confirm structure.

- [[Break of Structure]] — a candle close beyond a swing point; *confirms* the trend continues
- [[Change of Character]] — a candle close *against* the trend; *warns* of reversal
- [[Displacement Candle]] — the candle that creates a BOS or CHOCH; high-volume, large body
- [[Liquidity Sweep]] — often accompanies a structural event; the sweep triggers the close

---

## Directional Zones

Structure defines the range; ranges define where to enter.

- [[Higher Timeframe Bias]] — the 4H/1D structure read; the single most important filter
- [[Premium Zone]] — upper 50% of the range; short entries only in bearish structure
- [[Discount Zone]] — lower 50% of the range; long entries only in bullish structure
- [[Draw on Liquidity]] — structure points toward the next unswept liquidity pool

---

## Order Flow Zones — Structure Confirmed

Zones that form as a direct result of structural events.

- [[Order Block (Bullish)]] — valid only if the OB caused a structural break (BOS)
- [[Order Block (Bearish)]] — same requirement; the OB must have created a BOS
- [[Breaker Block]] — an OB broken by a structural BOS; flips polarity
- [[Mitigation]] — the OB is neutralized when structure returns to it on the first retest

---
