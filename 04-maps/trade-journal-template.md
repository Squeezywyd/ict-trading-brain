# Trade Journal Template

## Definition
The Trade Journal Template is the **standardized format for recording every trade taken**. A journal is not optional — it is the primary feedback loop that turns experience into improvement. Without logging, you repeat mistakes without knowing it.

Rule: **Every trade is logged, win or lose, within 30 minutes of closing.**

## Rules

### Entry to Log (Copy per Trade)

```markdown
## Trade Log — [DATE] [TIME GMT]

**Instrument:** (NQ, ES, EURUSD, etc.)
**Direction:** Long / Short
**Session:** London Open / NY Open / Other

### Setup
- HTF Bias (4H/1D): Bullish / Bearish
- Kill Zone: Active (London/NY) / Outside — (if outside, why did you trade?)
- Liquidity Sweep: Yes / No — Level swept: ___
- Displacement: Yes / No
- Entry Model: FVG / IFVG / OB / Breaker / Other
- Zone location: Discount / Premium / Neutral
- SMT Divergence: Yes / No / Not checked
- Confluence Score: ___ / 10

### Execution
- Entry Price: ___
- Stop Loss: ___ (distance: ___ points/pips)
- TP1: ___ (distance: ___ points/pips) | R:R to TP1: 1:___
- TP2: ___ | R:R to TP2: 1:___
- Position Size: ___ (% risk: ___)

### Outcome
- Result: Win (TP1) / Win (TP2) / Loss (SL) / Break-Even
- R:R Achieved: 1:___
- Exit Reason: TP hit / SL hit / Manual exit / Invalidation
- If manual exit: why? Was it correct? ___

### Review
- Did you follow all Entry Rules? Yes / No — What broke? ___
- Did you follow SL rules? Yes / No ___
- Emotions during trade: Calm / Anxious / Confident / Fearful
- What you did well: ___
- What to improve: ___
- Screenshot attached: Yes / No
```
