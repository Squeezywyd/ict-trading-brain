# Imbalances & Gaps - MOC

> **Purpose:** Everything about price imbalance — how it forms, how it gets traded, and when it flips polarity. FVGs and IFVGs are the two primary entry models in this system; this MOC maps the full concept space.

---

## The Imbalance Family

Parent concept and its specific subtypes, from most to least reliable.

- [[Imbalance]] — the root concept; any zone where buy/sell orders were not matched
- [[Fair Value Gap]] — the structured 3-candle imbalance; the primary entry model
- [[Volume Imbalance]] — the 2-candle close-to-open gap; weaker but still magnetic
- [[IFVG|Inverse Fair Value Gap]] — a broken FVG that has flipped polarity; reversal entry model
- [[Displacement Candle]] — the mechanism that *creates* all imbalances; no displacement = no gap
