# Sessions & Timing - MOC

> **Purpose:** The *when* of trading. ICT setups are not universal — the exact same pattern at 3 PM has a fraction of the probability of the same pattern at London open. This MOC maps every timing concept and how the three sessions interlock.

---

## The Three Sessions

Each session has a distinct character and role in the PO3 cycle.

- [[Asian Session Range]] — 20:00–00:00 EST; accumulation; mark the high and low, then wait
- [[London Open]] — 08:00–11:00 GMT; manipulation; highest probability sweep + reversal window
- [[NY Open]] — 13:30–16:00 GMT; distribution; big directional moves, especially near news
- [[Kill Zones]] — the three condensed windows (within sessions) where A+ setups form

---

## The PO3 Framework

How the three sessions fit into one repeating daily narrative.

- [[Power of Three]] — Accumulation (Asian) → Manipulation (London sweep) → Distribution (NY move)
- [[Liquidity Sweep]] — the PO3 "manipulation" phase; the sweep happens at London open
- [[Draw on Liquidity]] — the PO3 "distribution" target; where NY drives price
- [[Higher Timeframe Bias]] — determines the direction of the PO3 distribution phase

---

## Session Reference Levels

Levels created by sessions that become liquidity targets for the next one.

- [[Session Highs/Lows]] — each session's extremes become BSL/SSL for the next session
- [[Asian Session Range]] — the Asian high and low are London's primary sweep targets
- [[Buy-Side Liquidity]] — session highs accumulate BSL; marked before each new session
- [[Sell-Side Liquidity]] — session lows accumulate SSL; marked before each new session
- [[Equal Highs]] — session highs that match prior session highs = magnified BSL target
- [[Equal Lows]] — session lows that match prior session lows = magnified SSL target

## Timing Filters & Confirmation

How timing interacts with other entry factors.

- [[SMT Divergence]] — check at session open on correlated pairs (EURUSD/GBPUSD at London; NQ/ES at NY)
- [[Fair Value Gap]] — FVGs formed inside kill zone windows have the highest probability
- [[IFVG]] — IFVGs that form during a kill zone sweep carry maximum validity
- [[Pre-Trade Checklist]] — Phase 2 and 3 of the checklist are structured around session timing

- [[Kill Zones]] — outside these windows: no entries, no exceptions

- [[Worst Setups]] — midday FVG entries are catalogued here as a worst setup
